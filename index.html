About us

Originally created in 2018 as DuelingBook's Custom Cards Format by DuelingBook's staff members, Imaginary Industrial Illusions, or i3 for short, is a community that hosts Custom Card Tournaments with prize support in DuelingBook, while trying their best to satisfy the user experience. If you're bored of the current TCG metagame, simply looking for duels that don't have insanely overpowered custom cards, looking for balancing revisions by competent card makers and judges, or you just simply want to play in tournaments, you have come to the right place!

Our Staff

Administrators

The highest roles in the server. They are on charge of all things server-related. If you have any concerns about the management of the server, you can refer to them. They are also in charge of bans and changes to the server's banlist. Head Administrators are the administrator chiefs, and their decisions override everyone else below.

Card Adjudication

Super moderators of the server and individuals who approve/reject custom cards from our tournament format based on the server's balancing and design standards.

Card Managers

Individuals that are trusted with uploading and managing the card pool that is kept in the I3 DuelingBook account, in order to prevent unnoticed changes and malicious behavior.

Proofreaders

Individuals who do PSCT for cards within the server. Their main focus is text on approved custom cards. They are NOT required to make PSCT for custom cards that aren't approved. They DO NOT possess any moderation permissions, except managing messages in one specific room.

Card of the month

Auroral Witch, NeNe
Psychic / Synchro
Level 7
ATK 0 / DEF 2800
1 Tuner + 1+ non-Tuners
(Quick Effect): You can target 1 Effect Monster on the field; equip it to this card. Gains ATK equal to the combined original ATK of the monsters equipped to it by this effect. You can target 1 monster you control equipped to this card; Special Summon it. You can only use 1 "Auroral Witch, NeNe" effect per turn, and only once that turn.

A really powerful Level 7 disruption, that is also generic on top of it! Aurorals enter the competitive i3 scene with one of their several bosses providing a really powerful disruption. Use it to prevent your opponent's monsters from activating their effects, to steal a really powerful boss, to protect your own monsters from some effects, or even to boost NeNe herself!
